## Nobody Sees the Work

When you work at a company, your effort is visible. Colleagues notice when you stay late. Managers see your output. There is ambient social proof that you are a productive human.

When you build alone, you could work twelve hours in total silence and the world would remain completely indifferent.

The first time I shipped a feature I was genuinely proud of, I realized there was nobody to tell. I closed my laptop and went for a walk.

> Building in public helps, but it is a simulation of community, not the real thing.

The loneliness is real. Acknowledge it. Find your people — even asynchronously.
