## The Fake Door Test

A fake door test is simple: you build a landing page for a product that does not exist yet and see if people try to sign up or pay.

My landing page took four hours to build with Carrd. I described the product, listed pricing, and added a "Start Free Trial" button that led to a waitlist form.

I spent $50 on Twitter ads targeting my ideal customer profile.

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Clicks | 342 |
| Signups | 47 |
| Conversion rate | 13.7% |

A 13.7% conversion rate on a product that did not exist told me the demand was real. Only then did I start building.
