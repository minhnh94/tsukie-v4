## The Product Hunt Paradox

Product Hunt launches generate buzz, upvotes, and a spike of traffic from other indie hackers and tech enthusiasts.

They almost never generate revenue. Because that audience is not your customer.

My launch numbers:

| Metric | Day 1 | Week 1 |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Upvotes | 412 | 412 |
| Website visits | 1,847 | 2,103 |
| Sign-ups | 89 | 94 |
| Paying customers | 0 | 2 |

The two paying customers came from a Reddit post I wrote in a niche community three days after the launch. Total Reddit upvotes: 23.

The lesson: distribution is audience matching. Product Hunt is great for reaching the product-obsessed community. Find your actual customers somewhere else.
