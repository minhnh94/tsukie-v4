## The Productivity Ritual Industrial Complex

There is a whole industry built on selling you the perfect morning routine. Cold showers at 5am. Gratitude journals. Visualization. Wim Hof breathing.

I tried all of it. Some of it worked for a week. None of it became a lasting habit.

The problem with complex routines is they are brittle. One bad night's sleep and the whole system collapses. Then you feel guilty for breaking the streak, which makes everything worse.

![A simple notebook and pen on a desk](https://placehold.co/800x400/1e293b/38bdf8?text=Simple+Morning)

What actually worked: one non-negotiable. Every morning, before opening any app, I write three things I want to accomplish today. That is it. One habit, five minutes, zero friction.
