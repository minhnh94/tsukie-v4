## The Eight Months of Nothing

Month one: no revenue.
Month two: no revenue.
Month three: $9. One user, monthly plan, churned the next month.
Months four through eight: various amounts of nothing.

The internet makes it look like indie hackers go from zero to $1k MRR in 30 days. I watched those posts and wondered what I was doing wrong.

```
Revenue tracker (the honest version):
Jan: $0
Feb: $0
Mar: $9  ← peak excitement
Apr: $0  ← despair
May: $0
Jun: $27
Jul: $89
Aug: $234
Sep: $1,047 ← finally
```

What changed in September? Nothing specific. I had improved the product continuously, written 40 blog posts, answered hundreds of Reddit questions. The compound interest on effort finally tipped.
