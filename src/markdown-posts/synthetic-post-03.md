## Month Zero — The Leap

The day I submitted my resignation letter, I felt two things simultaneously: absolute terror and profound relief.

Everyone around me questioned the decision. My parents worried about healthcare. My colleagues thought I was having a breakdown. Maybe they were right.

But there is something clarifying about removing the safety net. When your runway is finite, priorities become crystal clear.

![A laptop on a desk with morning light streaming through a window](https://placehold.co/800x400/1e293b/38bdf8?text=The+Indie+Life)

The first three months were the hardest. Not because of the work — I loved the work — but because of the isolation and the silence where approval used to be.
