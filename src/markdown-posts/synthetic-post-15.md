## The Signal-to-Noise Problem

Hacker News has genuinely excellent technical content. The comments on a database paper can teach you more in 30 minutes than a week of tutorials.

But the site has a culture problem that nobody talks about: a chronic underestimation of human factors in technical decisions.

> "Why not just rewrite it in Rust?" — HN commenter, always

The best discussions happen when the topic is narrow and technical. The worst happen when the topic involves anything about how humans use software.

[Visit Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com)

My conclusion: read the technical threads, skip the product discussions, never read the comments on AI posts. Your sanity depends on it.
