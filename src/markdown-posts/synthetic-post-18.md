## The System Addiction

There is a certain type of person who builds elaborate productivity systems and never gets anything done. I was that person.

I had a beautiful Notion workspace, a detailed GTD implementation, color-coded calendars, time blocks, and a weekly review ritual. I also had a backlog of projects I had not touched in months.

The system felt like work. It looked like work. It was not work.

> The sign of a good productivity system is not that it is elegant. It is that work gets done and shipped.

The change: I deleted everything and wrote a single text file with three items. Every day, finish at least one.

It has been a year. I have shipped more in the last twelve months than in the previous three years combined.
